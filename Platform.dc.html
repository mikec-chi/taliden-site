01The enterprise AI gap

AI has intelligence. But does it understand how your business actually works?

A frontier model can generate, summarize, reason, and converse. But enterprise work requires knowing which order can ship, which purchase needs approval, which asset is critical, which policy applies, and which human is accountable. That knowledge does not live inside the model — it lives inside your business.

The enterprise AI lesson

Enterprise AI is not just a model. It is a system.

Large language models are becoming extraordinarily capable. But in the enterprise, raw intelligence is not enough. The first wave of AI asked which model to use. The next wave asks a harder question: how do we make AI understand our business, follow our rules, coordinate work, and execute safely?

Intelligence has to be wrapped in context, permissions, process, and accountability before it can touch real operations. That wrapper is the semantic operating layer — and it is what separates a demo from durable enterprise execution. Taliden is that layer.
