04Why it matters

Grounded in how your domain actually works.

Without semantic structure, AI relies on scattered documents, fragmented data, and prompt engineering. Taliden Graph gives AI an explicit model of the business — the meaning behind every object, state, and rule.

In transportation, it can model the difference between a live load and a preload, understand that a preload consumes yard capacity but not dock capacity at pickup, and validate scheduling recommendations against warehouse loading rules — before any agent acts.
